Ingredients:
- 3 avocados
- 2 TBsp. chopped onion
- 1 lime
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 whole jalapeno
 
Instructions:
- cut and mash avocados
- add onion
- dice the jalapenos
- squeeze lime
- add salt
- and mix well
